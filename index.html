Why Yachts & Living

Booking a yacht should feel easy. We make sure it is.

We are an agency based in Preveza. Every yacht in our fleet is carefully selected to meet our standards, and every crew member is trained and certified to meet the EU standards for yachting and safefty. When you book with us, you know exactly what you are getting.

Professional Crew, Every Time
Every captain and crew member in our fleet holds the required certifications. The yachts and catamarans we list have check their references, and stay in contact throughout the season. Your safety at sea is not negotiable.
Full Transparency on Costs
The charter rate, APA, VAT, and any port fees are laid out clearly before you sign anything. No surprises on your final bill. Your APA expenses are visible online in real time during the cruise.
Accountable from Day One
All charters run on MYBA contracts, the international gold standard for yacht charter agreements. You have legal protection, and we stand behind every booking we make.

How It Works

From enquiry to boarding in four steps.

01
Tell Us What You Need
Dates, group size, destination preference, and budget. We'll take it from there and come back with a curated shortlist of yachts.
02
We Match You to the Right Yacht
We present options with honest detail, including crew profiles, yacht condition, and what the APA realistically covers for your itinerary.
03
Sign and Secure
Your charter is confirmed with a MYBA contract and a 50% deposit. The remaining balance and APA are due one month before embarkation.
04
Board and Enjoy
We send full boarding instructions, itinerary suggestions, and stay on call throughout your trip. All APA spending is visible to you in real time.
